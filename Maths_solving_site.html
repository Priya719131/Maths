MATHS

Some basic operation in maths.

Addition

It includes the sum of two numbers. And it is represented by "+".
Examples:- let consider Number1=5 and Number2=6
Then the addition of 5 and 6 can be represented as
5 + 6 = 11

Subtraction

It includesdifference of two numbers. And it is represented by "-".
Examples:- let consider the Number1= 6 and Number2= 5
Then the Subtraction of 6 and 5 can be represented as
6 - 5 = 1.

Multiplication

It includes multiplication of two numbers. And it is represented by "*".
Examples:- let consider the Number1= 6 and Number2= 5
Then the Multiplication of 6 and 5 can be represented as
6 * 5 = 30.

Division

It includes division of two numbers. And it is represented by "/".
Examples:- let consider the Number1= 30 and Number2= 6
Then the Division of 30 and 6 can be represented as
30 / 6 = 5.
